Add a player (actor with strategic agency) to the analysis.
Inputs: `name` (required), `type` (state/organization/market/public/coalition/individual), `role` (primary/involuntary/background/gatekeeper/internal).
Players do not need sources at creation time, but every player's objectives and constraints should be grounded in evidence by the end of Phase 2.
Look beyond the obvious primary players — check for internal players (factions within a nominally unified actor), involuntary players (affected parties with constrained agency), and gatekeepers (who can block but not determine outcomes).
